In this case, the two sets are the hardcover books and the fiction books. Let H be the set of hardcover books and F be the set of fiction books. Then we want to find |H ∩ F|.

|H ∩ F| = 5

Therefore, the maximum number of books that are both hardcover and fiction is 5.
